# Kazi Skills

**Vision:** Building a future where your skill, not your background, determines your income.

## Uganda’s Youth Employment Challenge

There is a rapidly widening gap between available jobs and Uganda’s young workforce.

- **The Gap:** 700,000–800,000 youth enter the labor market annually, but the formal economy creates only about 90,000 jobs.
- **The Reality:** 80% are in vulnerable, informal employment. 16% of youth (ages 18–30) face unemployment, and 50% are classified as **NEET** (Not in Employment, Education, or Training).
- **The Impact:** Youth without practical skills fall into informal work, reinforcing generational poverty.

## Our Solution: Kazi Skills

Kazi Skills is an **AI-powered, mobile-first skills marketplace** that empowers Africa’s youth to learn practical skills and earn income directly from their phones.

- **Accessible Format:** 5–15 minute micro-learning videos aligned with short-form consumption habits, with offline watch capabilities.
- **Mobile-First:** Built specifically for low-end Android devices.
- **Relevant Categories:** High-demand skills such as Beauty & Makeup, Digital Hustles, and Food Business.
- **Clear Expectations:** Free 3-minute previews and upfront tool requirements for all learners.

## The Tech Advantage

- **Smart Discovery:** Natural-language search helps users find courses in English or Luganda.
- **Personalized Paths:** AI recommends courses based on career goals and individual progress.
- **Income Tracker:** Tracks competencies and suggests hyper-local gig and freelance opportunities.
- **Quality Control:** Automated AI flags low-quality or inappropriate creator videos.

## Target Audience & Market Expansion

We serve school leavers, informal workers (such as boda boda riders seeking digital side hustles), and rural youth without access to training centers.

- **Phase 1 (Months 1–6):** Kampala launch, expanding to Mbarara, Gulu, and Jinja.
- **Phase 2 (Months 7–18):** Uganda-wide rollout with localized Luganda content.
- **Phase 3 (Years 2–3):** East Africa expansion (Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Ethiopia) with Swahili content.

## 6-Month Roadmap & Outcomes

- **M1–M2 (Build & Test):** Finalize Android app, recruit 15 pilot creators, and validate with 50 beta users.
- **M3–M4 (Soft Launch):** Public launch in Uganda with 100 active courses and 500 learners onboarded.
- **M5–M6 (Scale & Learn):** Generate UGX 12M in course sales and reach 2,000 active learners.
